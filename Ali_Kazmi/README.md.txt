Description:

This script produces all of the included unique parking infractions from this spreadsheet.
in addition, it also:
- calculates the minimum, maximum and mean fine amounts of the different parking infractions combined.
- selects a unique parking infraction, which can be configured, and saves it in a new csv file.
- This files contents (the infraction information) are also printed for your review in the command line.
